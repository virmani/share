🃏 YES / NO Flip Cards — Print & Fold

Three sheets, one per person (Ashish, Nishka, Manasi). Each sheet folds in half to make a single double-sided card: green YES on one face, red NO on the other.

Print settings:

  1. Paper size: US Letter
  2. Print on one side (single-sided)
  3. Enable Background graphics / Print backgrounds (in the print dialog)
  4. Scale: 100% / Actual size (do not "Fit to page")
  5. Margins: None or Minimum

Fold: after printing, fold each sheet in half along the dashed line so the printed sides face outward. Folded card is 8.5″ × 5.5″ — readable from the back of a classroom.
